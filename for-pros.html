Our Process

A workflow built for coordination.

Seven clear checkpoints keep plans, scope, fabrication, scheduling, and installation aligned with the project team.

01Consultation

Confirm project goals, site context, timing, and team needs.

02Review Plans

Study drawings, specifications, and cabinetry requirements.

03Estimate & Scope

Clarify materials, allowances, schedules, and responsibilities.

04Shop Drawings

Prepare details for review before production begins.

05Fabrication

Build custom cabinetry and millwork with shop precision.

06Installation

Coordinate fit, delivery, site conditions, and sequencing.

07Final Walkthrough

Review finished work, details, and closeout expectations.
