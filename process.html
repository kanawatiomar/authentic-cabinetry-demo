Our Process

From first conversation to final walkthrough.

A simple process helps keep expectations, details, fabrication, and installation aligned from the beginning.

01Consultation

Share the project type, city, timeline, budget range, photos, plans, or inspiration images.

02Design

Review the room, use, measurements, finish direction, and details needed for the space.

03Fabrication

Cabinetry and millwork are built with attention to proportion, finish, and durability.

04Installation

Installation is coordinated with care for fit, job-site conditions, and communication.

05Final Walkthrough

The completed work is reviewed so final details and warranty information are clear.
