Company Story

Over 40 years of custom cabinetry and millwork experience.

Authentic Cabinetry is a custom cabinetry and millwork company proudly serving homeowners, designers, architects, builders, and businesses throughout the Bay Area and Northern California.

With over 40 years of craftsmanship and industry experience, Tarek Saati, Tim Moore, and the Authentic Cabinetry team specialize in high-quality custom cabinetry, built-ins, closets, vanities, architectural millwork, and storage solutions tailored to each client's vision.

The goal is simple: deliver exceptional craftsmanship, personalized service, and lasting value on every project.
